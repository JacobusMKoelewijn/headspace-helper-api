    General:
  • Best practice is to store all output .txt and CoA .pdf files in the diproma folder.

    • CoA files:
  • Upload up to 12 CoA files.
  • Use standardized format for all CoA files:
    "SolventAbreviation Manufacturer Catalog# Lot# ExpirationDate(mmmYYYY) purity(00.00%).pdf".
  • Example of correct CoA file:
    "EtOH Fisher E0650DF15 2034615 Feb2025 99.99%.pdf".
  • Solvent abbreviation used for the Headspace measurement should match with the CoA file!
  • For diluent use abbreviation: NMP, DMAC or DMI.
  • Heptanes is not supported n-heptane is.

    • Txt files:
  • Upload 8 to 12 A-files
  • Upload up to 8 B-files.
  • Upload up to 5 sample files.
  • Examples of correct sample formats:
    • XYZ12345-#-#-1_rest
    • XYZ12345678-#-#-1_rest
  • Examples of correct spiked sample formats:
    • XYZ12345-#-#-S-A1_rest
    • XYZ12345678-#-#-S-A1_rest
